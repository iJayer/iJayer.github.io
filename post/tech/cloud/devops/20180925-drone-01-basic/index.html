配置代码仓库的 OAuth

需要配置的参数：

  • DRONE_STASH=true
  • DRONE_STASH_GIT_USERNAME=OK
  • DRONE_STASH_GIT_PASSWORD=OK
  • DRONE_STASH_CONSUMER_KEY= ❌
  • DRONE_STASH_CONSUMER_RSA= ❌
  • DRONE_STASH_URL=http://stash.mycompany.com
